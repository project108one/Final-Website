Best Traditional Dishes in Bhutan

Top 3 dishes recommended by Sonam

Jasha Maru

Jasha maroo is a traditional Bhutanese dish consisting of diced chicken that is cooked with garlic, ginger, chiles, and tomato sauce.A chicken stew in a butter and simmered with spicy chilli served with rice

Suja

This unique beverage combines the richness of butter with the subtle bitterness of tea served hot, Suja is a perfect companion for chilly mountain evenings, offering a delightful balance of flavors and a glimpse into Bhutanese tea-drinking traditions.

Shakam Paa

This dish featuring dried beef infused with aromatic spices. The tender beef, marinated and sun-dried to perfection.It can go along with radish or dry chilli to make it more tastier.
